DirectX11
3D Game Engine

[Timeline: 2021.08 - 2022.02] | Role: Independent Developer

A 3D game engine built on DirectX 11 and Win32 interfaces. The engine implements contemporary game engine subsystems including Resource Management, Job System, and Render Graph, with an emphasis on low coupling and high extensibility.
